Grace D. Li

Grace D. Li grew up in Houston, Texas as the eldest daughter of two scientists. She is a graduate of Duke University, where she studied biology and creative writing, and currently attends medical school at Stanford University. Her writing has been recognized by the Saturday Evening Post Great American Fiction Contest and Stanford's Paul Kalanithi Writing Award, and has been published in Hyphen Magazine and The Intima. When she isn't writing or studying, she is searching for the best cheesecake in the Bay Area.
